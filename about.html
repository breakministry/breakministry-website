About Us

Break is a Catholic ministry that began in 2015 to help break the divide within the Catholic Church by offering contemporary music ministry to Catholic Churches, College Campus Ministries, and various Catholic Organizations. Over the years Break has continued to focus on breaking divides within the Catholic Church as a music ministry, but has expanded to now offer youth and young adult ministry consulting, a nightly online prayer group, and retreat support to help Catholich Parishes grow by leading parishioners to encounter Christ in the Eucharist and the Sacraments as well as encouraging Catholics to build an authentic, intentional, and lasting relationship with Christ. As our ministry has grown we have been blessed to offer support to the Diocese of Metuchen, the Diocese of Newark, and the Diocese of Trenton, Rutgers Catholic Campus Ministry, and Kean Catholic Campus Ministry in addition to parishes across New Jersey. Events we have done in the past include, but not limited to:

  • Retreats
  • Adoration Nights
  • Masses
  • Diocesan Events
  • Parish Events
  • Pilgrimages
  • College Worship Nights
